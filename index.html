The King Of Light Attack Helicopters

Affordable, agile, practical, and lethal, the AH-6 little bird does it all. Built on a 30 year history of special forces service and time tested AH-64 Apache avionics, the MH-6 Little Bird packs big punch in a small package. From combat search and rescue, to close air support and armed reconnaisance, the AH-6 is the solution you've been looking for.


Nicknamed "The Killer Egg" because of it's small size and fearsome capabilities, the AH-6 is capable of carrying an impressive array of weaponry. From rocket pods to miniguns, the Little Bird can be optomized for a wide variety of applications. Sporting a standard 61 gallon fuel tank, the AH-6 can be configured with two additional 31 gallon tanks and a 61 gallon goliath tank to ensure fuel and economically efficient operation. Used by militaries all over the world, the AH-6 continues to prove itself daily on missions across the globe. So what are you waiting for? Quick Facts:

  • Includes many features and avionics from the AH-64 Apache

  • Capable of carrying a max of 183 gallons of fuel for a max operational range of 331 km

  • Maximum rate of climb of 2,162 ft/min with a max operational cieling of 20,000 ft.

  • Maximum cruise speed of 126 knots

  • Capable of carrying four unique weapon systems with a max carrying capacity of 4 seperately attached weapon pods

  • Benefits from the ongoing efforts to modernize AH-64 Apaches
