The King Of Light Attack Helicopters

Affordable, agile, practical, and lethal, the AH-6 little bird does it all. Built on a 30 year history of special forces service and time tested AH-64 Apache avionics, the MH-6 Little Bird packs big punch in a small package. From combat search and rescue, to close air support and armed reconnaisance, the AH-6 is the solution you've been looking for.


Nicknamed "The Killer Egg" because of it's small size and fearsome capabilities, the AH-6 is capable of carrying an impressive array of weaponry. From rocket pods to miniguns, the Little Bird can be optomized for a wide variety of applications. Sporting a standard 61 gallon fuel tank, the AH-6 can be configured with two additional 31 gallon tanks and a 61 gallon goliath tank to ensure fuel and econically efficient operation. Used by militaries all over the world, the AH-6 continues to prove itself daily on missions across the globe. So what are you waiting for?
